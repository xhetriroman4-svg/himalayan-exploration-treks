## B. FEATURES & CAPABILITIES

### 1. SEARCH & FILTER SYSTEM
**Priority: CRITICAL — Without this, users cannot find trips**

- **Destination Search**: Text search with autocomplete/suggestions
- **Date Range Picker**: Flexible date selection with "flexible dates" option
- **Price Filter**: Min/max slider with currency toggle
- **Duration Filter**: Number of days/weeks range
- **Difficulty Filter**: Easy / Moderate / Challenging / Strenuous
- **Activity Type**: Trekking, Cultural, Wildlife, Adventure, Luxury, etc.
- **Group Size Filter**: Solo, small group (2-6), large group (7-15+)
- **Season/Month Filter**: Best time to visit
- **Rating Filter**: Minimum star rating
- **Availability Filter**: Show only trips with available spots
- **Sort Options**: Price, duration, rating, popularity, departure date
- **Save Search**: Save filter combinations for later
- **Search Results Count**: Real-time count as filters change

### 2. BOOKING SYSTEM
**Priority: CRITICAL — Core revenue driver**

- **Real-Time Availability**: Live spot availability per departure date
- **Availability Calendar**: Visual calendar with available/limited/sold-out indicators
- **Booking Form**: Multi-step form with progress indicator
- **Traveler Details**: Name, DOB, nationality, passport, dietary, medical
- **Guest Booking**: Option to book without creating account
- **Group Booking**: Book for multiple travelers in one transaction
- **Custom/Private Trips**: Request form for tailor-made itineraries
- **Waitlist**: Join waitlist for sold-out departures
- **Booking Modification**: Change dates, add travelers post-booking
- **Booking Cancellation**: Self-service cancellation with policy display
- **Installment Payments**: Split payment for expensive trips
- **Deposit Option**: Pay deposit now, balance later
- **Early Bird Discounts**: Automatic pricing for early bookings
- **Last-Minute Deals**: Automated discounting for near-term departures
- **Booking Confirmation**: Instant email + SMS confirmation
- **Booking Reference**: Unique alphanumeric reference per booking
- **Booking Status Tracking**: Confirmed → Pending → Completed → Cancelled

### 3. PAYMENT INTEGRATION
**Priority: CRITICAL**

- **Credit/Debit Cards**: Visa, Mastercard, Amex via Stripe/PayPal
- **Digital Wallets**: Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal
- **Bank Transfer**: Direct bank transfer with payment instructions
- **Multi-Currency**: Display and accept payments in multiple currencies
- **SSL Encryption**: 256-bit SSL for all transactions
- **PCI Compliance**: PCI-DSS Level 1 compliance
- **Payment Receipts**: Automated receipt generation
- **Invoice Generation**: Downloadable invoices for business travelers
- **Partial Payments**: Support for deposits and installments
- **Refund Processing**: Automated and manual refund workflows
- **Tax Handling**: VAT/GST calculation and display per jurisdiction

### 4. TRIP COMPARISON
**Priority: HIGH**

- **Side-by-Side View**: Compare 2-3 trips simultaneously
- **Comparison Criteria**: Price, duration, difficulty, included/excluded, itinerary, altitude, accommodation
- **Visual Comparison**: Charts/graphs for price and altitude comparison
- **Save Comparison**: Bookmark or share comparison link

### 5. INTERACTIVE MAPS
**Priority: HIGH**

- **Global Destination Map**: World map with all destinations marked
- **Trip Route Map**: Day-by-day route with waypoints
- **Altitude Profile Map**: Elevation chart overlay on route
- **Points of Interest**: Hotels, attractions, hospitals, embassies
- **Offline Map Access**: Download maps for offline use
- **Map Clustering**: Group nearby markers at zoom-out levels

### 6. WEATHER INFORMATION
**Priority: HIGH**

- **Current Conditions**: Real-time weather at destination
- **Seasonal Weather Charts**: Monthly temperature, rainfall, humidity averages
- **Best Time to Visit**: Visual indicator of optimal travel months
- **Weather Alerts**: Severe weather warnings
- **Trail Conditions**: For trekking — trail open/closed, snow conditions

### 7. REVIEWS & RATINGS
**Priority: HIGH**

- **Star Rating System**: 1-5 star overall + sub-ratings (guide, accommodation, food, value)
- **Written Reviews**: Detailed text reviews with date and trip name
- **Photo Reviews**: User-uploaded trip photos with reviews
- **Video Testimonials**: Embedded video reviews
- **Review Verification**: Verified badge for confirmed travelers only
- **Review Response**: Company replies to reviews
- **Review Sorting**: By date, rating, helpfulness
- **Review Filtering**: By trip, traveler type, rating
- **Aggregate Score**: Overall company rating displayed site-wide
- **Third-Party Integration**: TripAdvisor, Google Reviews, Trustpilot widgets

### 8. WISHLIST / FAVORITES
**Priority: MEDIUM-HIGH**

### 9. LIVE CHAT SUPPORT
**Priority: HIGH**

### 1. TRIP PLANNER
**Functionality:**

### 2. BUDGET CALCULATOR
**Functionality:**
- Input: Destination, duration, travel style (budget/mid-range/luxury), number of travelers
- Output: Estimated costs broken down by:
  - Flights
  - Accommodation
  - Food & drinks
  - Activities & tours
  - Transportation (local)
  - Visa & documentation
  - Insurance
  - Shopping & miscellaneous
- Comparison: Budget vs. mid-range vs. luxury
- Savings tips per category
- Export/download budget plan

**Functionality:**

- Offline mode with last-known rates
- Tip calculator per country
- Currency comparison table for multi-destination trips

### 7. DISTANCE CALCULATOR
**Functionality:**
- Input two locations → Output distance (km/mi), flight time, driving time
- Show route on map
- Multiple stops routing
- Elevation gain calculation for trekking routes
- Compare distances across different routes

### 8. TREK DIFFICULTY ESTIMATOR

  - Risk assessment
